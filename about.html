About of Bia's Kitchen Catering

This business started because of our passion for cooking delicious food because we come from the province of

Pampanga. My inspiration for building a catering services business is my grandfather because he also loves to

eat and we along well, but unfortunately he has passed away. Far beyond a mere business, this journey is personal

to me. My grandfather was my biggest inspiration-his love for food gave birth to some of our best memories shared.

Every single meal we prepare is imbued with love and dedication, inspired from my grandfather.

Through our catering services, we hope to connect people, to create memories, and keep alive

the tradition of sharing beautiful food with the ones that matter most.
